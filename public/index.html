About

UniVibe is a digital space built for students who are tired of scattered information and missed announcements. We unify everything happening in your college into one clean dashboard, from official notices to club events, internship announcements, lost & found, and guidance from seniors.
This space makes campus life smoother, smarter, and more connected.
